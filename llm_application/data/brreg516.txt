



Download the app
You must download the app My hunting documentation before undergoing the shooting test.
You will find the app in the Google Play shop or the App store.
The first time you log in to the app through ID-porten it requires mobile coverage.
Create a personal code in the app
You will be asked to create a four-digit code, so that you later can use the app without mobile coverage. Remember your code when you go hunting.
In the app, you will see if you have paid the hunting licence fee and find the weapons that you have stated at My hunting profile.
The new app collects the hunting licence and diploma for big game hunting test in one place.
