



Make changes to your condominium flat owner

Last updated: 31 March 2022.





If you wish to make changes to a condominium flat owner, you do this by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.

                                How do I register a new board?                            



You register a new board by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.
The board must be elected by the annual meeting.
You must attach

the minutes from the annual meeting showing the decision

Upon registration in the Register of Business Enterprises 

the board members must confirm taking on the assignment











 
                                How do I register a new signature right?                            



When the condominium changes signature, you must report this in the form Coordinated register notification.
You must attach

minutes from the annual meeting showing the assignment of signature right

and

updated articles of association containing the  signature provision











 
                                How do I register an auditor?                            



You register an auditor by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.
The auditor must be elected by the annual meeting, and confirm taking on the assignment by singing the form.
You must attach

the minutes from the annual meeting showing the decision











 
                                How do I register an accountant?                            



You register an accountant by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.










 
                                How do I resign my role in the enterprise?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation. When you resign, you waive all rights and obligations belonging to your role. You have to notify us about your resignation through the form Coordinated register notification. Once the resignation has been registered, both the condominium and you who withdraw will be notified of this.
