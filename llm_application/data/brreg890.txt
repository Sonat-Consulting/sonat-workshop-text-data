



Subscription to announcements in XML format

Last updated: 18 January 2023.





You may subscribe to daily registrations of announcements.

You will be supplied with announcements from the Register of Business Enterprises, the Register of Bankruptcies, the Register of Company Accounts, the Register of Political Parties and the Foundation Authority. These announcements are available in XML format, and you can decide which type of announcement you wish to subscribe to.
If you want to build a database with the same advertising texts that are covered by the announcement solution, you can subscribe to this.
 







 
                                How to place an order                            



You can take out a subscription by ticking off for the announcement types you wish to include in the subscription. The subscription list can be found under “Agreements” along with the Standard agreement which must be completed and submitted. The delivery begins when we have received a completed/signed agreement.










 
                                Fees and terms                            



The fee depends on the number of announcement types in the subscription list.
The subscription list is enclosed in the agreement between the Brønnøysund Register Centre and the subscriber.










 
                                Agreements                            




Terms of subscription are regulated by the standard agreement
You need to sign and submit both the standard agreement and Appendix 1 to access the service
You can submit the agreements via e-mail or mail.

Standard agreement announcements (PDF) – in Norwegian
Subscription list (PDF) – in Norwegian
 










 
                                Tecnical description                            




A zip file containing one file per announcement for each announcement type will be delivered. If ten new companies are announced registered, you will be supplied with ten new registration (NYFR)files.
The delivery takes place by creating an sftp account

If you want a technical description of how the files are built up, we can send you XSD files and XML showing this.










 
                                Contact us                            



For further details about the service, please send us an e-mail.
