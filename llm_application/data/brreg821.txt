



Youth enterprise

Last updated: 23 November 2022.





Before you register the youth enterprise with us, you must register it with Ungt Entreprenørskap.

Upon registration of the youth enterprise, you must have the following information available:

name of the youth enterprise
date of incorporation
address
language variant
contact person

In addition, you can register signature rights, which is voluntary information.
Please remember to submit:

memorandum of association
articles of association

Ungt Entreprenørskap has its own website, ungdomsbedrift.no, to guide you in starting, running and managing a youth enterprise. In addition, you get answers to many other questions about youth enterprise.

Would you like to check what is registered about your youth enterprise?
You can check the information on Key information from the Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities.
Closure of the youth enterprise
When you want to close down the youth enterprise, you must submit the form for striking off an enterprise. You must enclose minutes from the liquidation meeting. The contact person must sign the notification.
You will find more about closure on the website of Ungt Entreprenørskap.
