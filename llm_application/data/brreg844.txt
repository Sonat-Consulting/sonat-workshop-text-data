



Announcements from the Register of Political Parties

Last updated: 21 December 2022.





This register announces decisions on registration of new party names, changes of party names, merging of parties under a new name, as well as decisions from the Board of Appeals. 

The announcements will normally be available three days after the information has been registered in the Register of Political Parties.
 







 
                                Announcement of registration of a new party name                            



The announcement contains name and organisation number of the party.










 
                                Announcement of change of a party name                            



The announcement contains information about:

new and previous party name
organisation number
the expiry date for the exclusive rights to the previous party name











 
                                Announcement of merging of parties under a new name                            



The announcement contains information about:

new party name
previous party name and organisation number for the parties involved in the merger
expiry date for its exclusive rights or the previous party names











 
                                Announcement of striking off a party name                            



The announcement contains information about:

the party name that has been struck off
whether the party is still registered in the Register of Political Parties under another name, as well as the organisation number of the party, if any
further information about the reason for the deletion of the party name











 
                                Announcement about decision from the Board of Appeals                            



The announcement contains information about the fact that the Board of Appeals has made a decision, with a reference to the name and organisation number of the party the decision applies to.










 
                                Where do I find the announcements?                            



If you know the name of the party, you can search directly for the name or organisation number on our announcement pages. Alternatively, you can search through advanced search. Select announcement type –  Political parties.
