



Reporting obligations to the Register of Company Accounts

Last updated: 28 February 2022.





All enterprises with an accounting obligation must submit annual accounts to the Register of Company Accounts no later than one month after it has been approved.

Non-calendar fiscal year










The main rule is that the fiscal year follows the calendar year.
The accounting obligation applies from the date of incorporation, even if there has not been any activity.
The obligation applies until the business is struck off. You submit the liquidation accounts after the activity is registered closed.
