



About the Municipal Reporting Register (KOR)

Last updated: 14 April 2023.





The Municipal Reporting Register (KOR) provides an overview of the reporting obligations that  government agencies have imposed on municipalities and county municipalities. 

KOR is a tool for coordination functions in the KOSTRA system and a means of coordination and simplification. The registry is to be updated and contain a complete overview of and information about the reporting obligations of the municipal sector.
The Brønnøysund Register Centre operates KOR on behalf of the Ministry of Local Government and Regional Development , and is responsible for the quality of KOR.
