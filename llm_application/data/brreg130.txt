



Subscription to information from the Register of Mortgaged Moveable Property

Last updated: 30 July 2021.





You can subscribe to periodic transcripts from the Register of Mortgaged Moveable Property and the Aquaculture Register 

This applies to credit inquiry agencies requiring a licence to do business.
The service comprises delivery of most of the document types recorded in the Register of Mortgaged Moveable Property and the Aquaculture Register. They can be recorded on organisation number, national identity number/d-number and registration number-/approval number.
 







 
                                Fees and terms                            




The fee for one year of updates from the Register of Mortgaaged Moveable Property is NOK 100.000,00.
The fee for one year of updates from the Aquaculture Register is NOK 24.000,00.
The terms of delivery of customised products are regulated by a standard framework agreement.











 
                                Agreements                            




Terms of subscription are regulated by the standard framework agreement
You need to sign and submit both the framework agreement and a sub-agreement to access the service
You can submit the agreement via e-mail or mail.

Framework agreement (PDF) – in Norwegian
Sub-agreement regarding the Register of Mortgaged Moveable Property (PDF) – in Norwegian










 
                                Technical description                            



You will be supplied with periodic transcripts of daily updates in an SFTP account. The update files are based on a safe section length. In this link you will find a complete description and list of the included document types (PDF) – in Norwegian.
