



Establish and register a house building cooperative

Last updated: 9 March 2022.





Here you can register a house building cooperative, and you can see what requirements apply to the foundation and the registration.

Coordinated register notification








A house building cooperative has to register in the Register of Business Enterprises. The deadline is three months from the founding document has been signed by the founders.
In order to establish a house building cooperative, the founders must create a memorandum of association
The founders are those who are entitled to subscribe for the shares in the cooperative. It must be at least twenty shareholders and subscription shall be made in the foundation document. Natural persons can own only one share each, while legal persons, such as a limited company, can own several shares.
The shares must be at least NOK 300, but not greater than NOK 5,000 and the share capital at the foundation must be at least NOK 100,000. The articles of association may stipulate that the shares can have different nominal value.
Register the house building cooperative
When reporting a house building cooperative, you need to enclose

memorandum of association
articles of association
opening balance sheet
confirmation from the auditor that the opening balance sheet has been prepared in accordance with the provisions of the Accounting Act
statement from the auditor confirming that he or she has taken on the assignment
statement from the auditor confirming that the capital has been paid-up

The board members must through the form Coordinated register notification confirm that they have taken on the assignment. The same must be done by an accountant, if any.
 







 
                                Requirements for the memorandum of association                            



The memorandum of association must contain a minimum of information, such as

the name of the house building cooperative
the articles of association
name and national ID number or organisation number of all the founders
information about who has been elected to the board and auditor
an overview of the owners of the shares

When all the founders have signed the memorandum of association, the shares are subscribed and the cooperative is founded.










 
                                  Requirements for the articles of association                            



The statutes must contain a minimum of information, such as

the name of the house building cooperative, which must contain the word house building cooperative or the abbreviation BBL
the municipality where the enterprise is to have its registered business office
what type of activity the cooperative is operating in addition to the main activity
nominal value of the shares
the minimum and the maximum number of board members
the issues that the annual general meeting must deal with

approval of the annual account and the annual report
other matters which according to the law or the statutes belong to the general meeting
