



Trainee positions – Candidate Helgeland

Last updated: 23 August 2022.





Nyutdannet og eventyrlysten? 

We collaborate with the Helgeland Knowledge Park about trainee positions within IT system development, through the trainee programme Candidate Helgeland.
To be eligible to apply, you need to have a bachelor’s or master’s degree from a university/academy. In addition, you must not have more than three years’ relevant work experience after completing your studies, or not be older than 32 years. As a trainee you may also participate in a competence building programme containing practical management courses, business visits, ambassadour courses and study tours.  The application deadline is on 1 March each year.
