



Business statistics

Last updated: 9 May 2023.





The Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities and the Register of Business Enterprises are responsible for registration of all the Norwegian and foreign enterprises running a business in Norway.

Here you find statistics showing new registrations and total inventory from the Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities and the Register of Business Enterprises
See interactive visualisation of the statistics at the websites of Tableau.





Charts showing the development in the number of new registrations of businesses.


If you wish complete statistics, you can find it here:
You can download statistics from the Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities and the Register of Business Enterprises in Excel format. You can choose how to sort the information, and you have the possibility to create your own statistics based on the criteria.
