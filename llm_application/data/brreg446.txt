



How to register a foundation

Last updated: 28 February 2022.





All foundations must be registered in the Foundation Register

The board is responsible for reporting the registration within three months of its establishment. In order to register a foundation, you must submit the form Coordinatd register notification.

The Brønnøysund Register Centre provides the foundation with a preliminary organisation number before we forward all the documents for processing at the Foundation Authority.
Attachment requirements
When registering a foundation, you must submit several documents. You will find an overview of these and what information they should contain at the webpage auxiliary forms and templates of the Founding Authority.
