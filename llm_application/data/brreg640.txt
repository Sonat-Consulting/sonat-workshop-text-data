



Articles of association of a limited company

Last updated: 17 August 2022.





The memorandum of association must include the articles of association.

Items the articles of association must contain

the business name
the objectives of the business
the amount of the share capital
the nominal value of the share

If the company’s objective does not intend to provide the shareholders with a financial dividend, the articles of association must contain provisions on how the company should distribute surplus and assets when the company is dissolved. If the company is to have several general managers, this must also be stated in the statutes.
 




Applicable rules

The Limited Liability Companies Act





The Business Enterprise Registration Act
