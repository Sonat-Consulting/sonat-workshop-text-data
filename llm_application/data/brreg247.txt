



Roles in the enterprise

Last updated: 21 October 2021.





This is an open API with role information from the Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities and the Register of Business Enterprises.

You must state an organisation number in order to retrieve information about all the role holders in the business in question. We can provide information about the name and the birth date on a role holder. The overview will show the total number of roles, regardless of whether they are private individuals or enterprises.
