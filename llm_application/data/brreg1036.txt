



Full access to the Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities

Last updated: 27 April 2023.





We provide full technical access to both private and public actors.

This service is free of charge for public actors, while private actors will be charged with a fee.
Today all public actors are obliged to re-use and coordinate information from the Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities.
 







 
                                Fees                            



Private actors are charged with a yearly fee in fixed expenses of NOK 100.000,00. An extra cost per search of NOK 1.00 will be added for

basic data
roles
power of procuration
group
multiple subentities
one subentity

The fee for a role overview is NOK 10.00.
The amounts will be invoiced annually and half-yearly.










 
                                Content of the service and technical descrption                            



You are responsible for developing the applications you would like to use to import data from the Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities. We do not provide technical assistance in this area, but we can provide expert advice regarding basic data from the register.
Here you will find more information.










 
                                How to gain access                            



Submit an e-mail to receive the agreements.
When you have signed an agreement, you will be assigned a username and a password that you need to apply in order to be linked up to the solution.










 
                                Contact us                            



For further details about the service, please send us an e-mail.
