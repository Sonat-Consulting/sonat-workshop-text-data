Strike off a joint ownership according to the law
Coordinated register notification








A joint ownership ceases to exist if the co-ownership is sold or terminated, or if only one owner remains.
The co-owners can also decide to strike off the registered enterprise.
You have to attach

documentation showing that the joint ownership has ceased to exist, or the minutes from the meeting of joint owners showing the decision to close the enterprise
