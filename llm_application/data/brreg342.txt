



Revoking a demerger of co-operatives

Last updated: 6 November 2021.





Decision to demerge co-operatives can be revoked by the annual meeting. 

The revoking has to be approved either by the aquired company or by all the aquiring companies.
You must attach

the minutes from the annual meeting containing the decision to revoke the demerger
