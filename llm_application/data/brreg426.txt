



Submission of annual accounts for non-profit organisations

Last updated: 28 February 2022.





Some non-profit organisations must submit annual accounts. 







Submit annual accounts
Submission form, Altinn















Submit annual accounts
Submission form, Altinn









Associations which are registered in the Register of Non-Profit Organisations, and have undertaken an obligation to report accounting records, must each year submit annual accounts to the Register of Company Accounts.
The annual accounts must contain

accounts of activities
balance sheet
note disclosures
annual report

You submit the annual accounts through a separate form in Altinn.
The deadline for the submission of the annual accounts is:

30 September for annual accounts whose fiscal year ends  in the period 1 July – 31 December
31 March for annual accounts whose fiscal year ends in the period 1 January to 30 June

The Register of Non-Profit Organisations will notify associations which do not meet the reporting obligations.
