



Register the weapon
Before you can undergo the shooting test, you must register the weapon you are going to use for the test.
In order to register the weapon you need to

Log on to My hunting profile through ID-porten
Consent that we may store your information about weapons
Select register a new weapon, enter the name of the weapon, caliber and weapon number and any barrel number
Save the information, and the weapon is ready for the shooting test

You will now find an overview of your information stored under registered weapons. You can delete the weapon until the shooting test is approved.
