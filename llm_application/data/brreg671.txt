



Make changes to your public limited company

Last updated: 18 August 2022.





Any changes of the registered information, must be reported to the Register of Business Enterprises. You do this by submitting the form Coordinated register notification in Altinn. 









 
                                How do I report a new business name?                            



You register a new business name by submitting the form Coordinated register notification. It is the general meeting that adopts a new business name. The change requires a change in the articles of association.
You have to attach

minutes from the general meeting
updated articles of association

On the page How do I choose a name for my enterprise? you can read more about the choice of names and what considerations you need to take.










 
                                How do I report a new address?                            



You can register a new address by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.
New business address
The business address for a public limited company has to be a Norwegian street address or name of place.
When changing the address to a new municipality, the company’s statutes must be changed. The general meeting has to approve such a change.
Upon a change to a new municipality, you must attach

articles of association
minutes form the general meeting showing the decision to amend the articles of association

New postal address
You are not required to report a separate postal address. If you would like your mail sent to a different address than the business address, you can report it as postal address.










 
                                How do I report a new board?                            



When the company has elected a new board, you must report this through the form Coordinated register notification. The new board members must consent to the registration by signing the form.
The general meeting must elect the board.
You must attach

minutes from the general meeting showing the decision
any minutes/document showing the employees being elected to the board











 
                                How do I report a new general manager?                            



When the company hires a general manager, you must report this in the form Coordinated register notification.
There are usually no requirements for attachments.
The exception is if you as a general manager submits a notification and you do not have roles in the board. Then you must attach

documentation showing the employment, such as minutes from a board meeting











 
                                How do I report new signature rights?                            



When the company changes the signature right, you must report this in the form Coordinated register notification.
You must attach

minutes from the board meeting showing the assignment of signature, unless the entire board has signed in the form

It must be clearly stated that the signature right is assigned, and whether it is assigned jointly or separately.










 
                                How do I report a new auditor?                            



Once the company has elected a new auditor, you report this through the form Coordinated register notification.
The general meeting must elect the auditor. The auditor must confirm taking on the assignment by signing in the form.
You must attach

minutes from the general meeting showing the resolution











 
                                How do I resign my role in the company?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation. When you resign, you waive all rights and obligations belonging to your role. You have to notify us about your resignation through the form Coordinated register notification. Once the resignation has been registered, both the  company and you will be notified of this.
