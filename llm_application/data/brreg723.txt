



How to register a co-operative

Last updated: 26 August 2022.





A co-operative has to register in the Register of Business Enterprises.







Register a co-operative

Register a co-operative 
Coordinated register notification









The enterprise must register within three months from the signing of the memorandum. In order to register the enterprise, you have to fill in and submit the form Coordinated register notification.
Attachment requirements
There are several types of documents you have to attach when registering a co-operative.
Documents you always have to attach when registering an enterprise are

the memorandum of association, including the statutes

When depositing money you must enclose

a declaration from an auditor or a financial institution confirming the payment of the contributed capital

If the contribution consists of non-liquid assets, you have to attach

the opening balance sheet, signed by the founders
a declaration from an auditor confirming the payment of the contributed capital

If the enterpriseâ€™s object includes banking or insurance activity, you have to attach

approval from the Financial Supervisory Authority of Norway

Please remember to sign the form
Members of the board and any auditor and accountant must confirm that they have taken on the assignment. They do this by signing the form Coordinated register notification.
