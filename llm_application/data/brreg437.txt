



Frequently asked questions about annual accounts and late filing penalty

Last updated: 28 February 2022.













 
                                I cannot download the decision in Altinn, what can I do?                            



You have to install the latest version of Adobe Reader.
You can switch to another browser, such as Google Chrome.










 
                                Who needs to sign the annual accounts and the report?                            



Pursuant to the law all board members or partners and the general manager, if any, have to sign.
The Register of Company Accounts will still approve the annual accounts without the signatures, because signing in Altinn meet the signature requirement.










 
                                Can I delete already submitted accounts which contain errors?                            



No, you have to submit a new version of the accounts.










 
                                I have received an invoice from the National Collection Agency. Why?                            



You will find the answer under late filing penalty.










 
                                I will not be able to pay the invoice from the National Collection Agency, what can I do?                            



You can contact the National Collection Agency to be granted a deferment or repayment schedule.










 
                                I am no longer a member of the board, what can I do?                            



You can report that you are resigning from a role through the form Coordinated register notification in Altinn.










 
                                What are notes, and what should they contain?                            



Notes are supplementary information to the accounts.
Chapter 7 of the Accounting Act states what the notes should contain. You must at least disclose the number of employees, loans and loan security to leading persons, shareholders etc. Limited companies must in addition inform about the number of shares, shareholders and its own shares.










 
                                It is not possible to upload file attachments when I am trying to deliver the accounts, what can I do?                            



You can switch to another browser, such as Google Chrome.










 
                                How much can the late filing penalty amount to?                             



Here you will find an overview of how the late filing penalty is calculated










 
                                What are small businesses?                            



Small businesses include enterprises with

revenues below NOK 70 million
balance sheet total of less than NOK 35 million
number of employees under 50 man-years

You can read more about this in section 1-6 of the Accounting Act.










 
                                My annual accounts have not been approved, how do I submit it once more?                            



You can use the function Create a new copy in Altinn. You will find the form in question under “Archived” in “Inbox”.
Change the information which is incorrect in the form, delete or add the required file attachments and submit once more.
If the accounts are sent through an accounting programme, any new submittal should be done through this programme.










 
                                Can you help me with the tax return or the income statement?                            



No, you must contact the Tax Administration if you have any questions about the tax return or the income statement.










 
                                Can you help me keeping accounts?                            



No, you must contact an accountant if you need help with keeping accounts.
