



Make changes to your joint ownership according to the law of property

Last updated: 21 October 2022.





If you wish to make changes to the information registered on a joint ownership, you must submit the form Coordinated register notification. 







 Report changes
Coordinated register notification

                                How do I report a new address?                             



You register a new address by submitting the form Coordinated register notification. You can choose whether to change the business address or the postal address.
If you want the joint ownership to only be registered with a postal address with us, please remember to remove the old business address in the form.
If you want to use the address of one of the co-owners as the address of the joint ownership, you must use a c/o address. See the example below:
Sameiet Sommerro
c/o Kari Nordmann
Utsikten 54
8908 Brønnøysund










 
                                How do I register a new general manager, contact person or business manager?                            



When the joint ownership has got a new general manager, contact person or business manager, you must submit the new information in the form the Coordinated register notification.
No attachments are required.










 
                                 How do I report a new board?                            



When the joint ownership has elected a new board, you must submit the form Corrdinated register notification.
You must attach

minutes from the meeting of joint owners, showing the election of the board











 
                                How do I register new signature rights?                            



You submit the form Coordinated register notification in order to change who has signature right in the joint ownership.
You must attach

unanimous agreement between the co-owners
or
articles of association that has been unanimously adopted

It must be clear from the documentation that the signature has been assigned and whether it is given jointly or separately.










 
                                How do I resign my role in the joint ownership?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation. When you resign, you waive all rights and obligations belonging to your role. You have to notify us about your resignation through form Coordinated register notification. Once the resignation has been registered, both the joint ownership and the person resigning will be notified about this.
