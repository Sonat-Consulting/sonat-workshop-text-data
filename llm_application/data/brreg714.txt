



Make changes to your general partnership?

Last updated: 25 August 2022.





If you want to register new information on your business, you will find approaches for different changes here.







Report changes
Coordinated register notification















Report changes
Coordinated register notification

















 
                                How do I report a new address?                            



You can change your address by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.
New business address
The business address for a partnership has to be a Norwegian street address or name of place.
If you change the address to another municipality, you must form a new partnership agreement. Unless the new partnership agreement has been signed by all the partners, the partnership meeting has to approve the change.
When changing to another municipality, please enclose

an updated partnership agreement
minutes from the partnership meeting, unless the new partnership agreement has been signed by all the partners

New postal address

You are not obliged to report a separate postal address. If you want to have your mail sent to another address, you can register it as a postal address.










 
                                How do I report new partners?                            



You report change of partners by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.
You have to form a new partnership agreement. If all the partners have not signed the partnership agreement has not been signed after the change, the partnership meeting has to approve the change.
You must attach

an updated partnership agreement
minutes from the partnership meeting unless the partnership agreement has been signed by all the partners
declarations from the partners that resign, where they consent to the fact that the resignation will be registered in The Register of Business Enterprises











 
                                How do I report a new board?                            



You register a new board by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.
The board is elected by the partnership meeting
You must attach

minutes from the partnership meeting showing the decision











 
                                How do I report a new business name?                            



You register a new name by submitting the form Coordinated register notification. You have to form a new partnership agreement.
Minutes from the partnership meeting must be enclosed unless all the partners have signed the partnership agreement.
You must attach

updated partnership agreement
minutes from the partnership meeting, unless all the partners have signed the partnership agreement

On the page How do I choose a name for my company? you can read more about the choice of names and what considerations you need to take.










 
                                How do I resign my role in the company?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation. When you resign, you waive all rights and obligations belonging to your role. You have to notify us about your resignation through the form Coordinated register notification. Once the resignation has been registered, both the company and you will be notified of this.










 
                                How do I report a new signature right?                            



When the company changes the signature, you must report this in the form Coordinated register notification.
You must attach

minutes from the partnership meeting showing the assignment of signature

It must be clearly stated from the minutes that the signature is assigned, and whether it is assigned jointly or separately.










 
                                How do we change our general partnership into an AS?                            



You cannot register a direct conversion from a general partnership into a limited company. You must therefore

register the new limited company
strike off the general partnership

In order for the conversion to be tax-free, the limited company must be established and notification of this sent to the Register of Business Enterprises during the period 1 January to the end of June in the income year, and tax deductions and employer’s National Insurance contributions are calculated and made no later than 1 July. Read more about tax conversion under Start and run business in Altinn.
