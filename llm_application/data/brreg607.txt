



Announcements from the Register of Bankruptcies




The Register of Bankruptcies announces decisions about commencement, all types of changes, discontinuation, completion and continuation of receivership.

The announcements will normally be available on the same day as the bankruptcy order has been made.
 







 
                                Commencement of bankruptcy proceedings                            



The announcement includes information about

the organisation number/the date of birth and name of the debtor
the address
the branch/industrial code
which district court has commenced the bankruptcy proceeding
the commencement date
the case number
the name and address of the official receiver
the time limit for filing claims on the estate
the closing date
the time and the venue for the first meeting of creditors











 
                                Compulsory liquidation order                            



The announcement includes information about

the organisation number and name of the debtor
the address
the branch/industrial code
which district court has made the compulsory liquidation order
the commencement date
the case number
the name and address of the official receiver
the time limit for filing claims on the estate
the closing date
the time and venue for the first meeting of creditors











 
                                Compulsory dissolution order                            



The announcement includes information about

the organisation number and name of the debtor
the address
the branch/industrial code
which district court has made the compulsory dissolution order
the commencement date
the case number
the name and address of the official receiver
the time limit for filing claims on the estate
the closing date
the time and venue for the first meeting of creditors











 
                                Change of announcement from the Register of Bankruptcies                            



The announcement includes information about what has been changed in connection with previously announced bankruptcy orders. This can be a change of official receiver, a change of meeting of creditors, changes in previously announced deadlines, etc.










 
                                Termination of bankruptcy/compulsory liquidation/compulsory dissolution                            



An order for the commencement of bankruptcy/compulsory liquidation/compulsory dissolution may be appealed to the Court of Appeal. If the Court of Appeal annuls the order, the termination order will be available in the announcement database for three weeks.
The announcement includes information about

the organisation number/the date of birth, name and address of the debtor
the date when the termination order was made
which Court of Appeal has made it
which district court that originally made the order to commence bankruptcy/compulsory liquidation
a reference to the district courtâ€™s original case number











 
                                Receivership stopped                            



The announcement includes information about

the organisation number/date of birth and name of the debtor
the address
which district court terminated the receivership for the estate
the date
the case number
the time limit for appeals











 
                                Termination of receivership                            



The announcement includes information about

the organisation number/date of birth and name of the debtor
the address
which official receiver has wound up the estate
the dividend priority and payment
the date of distribution
the case number
the time limit for appeals











 
                                Continuation of receivership                            



The announcement includes information about

the organisation number/date of birth and name and address of the debtor
the case number
the district court that has decided that the receivership should continue
the name and address of the official receiver

Continuation of receivership is automatically announced unless the district court stipulates otherwise.










 
                                Where do I find the announcements?                            



If you know the name of the company, you can search directly for the name or organisation number at our announcement pages. Otherwise, you can search through advanced search, announcement type Bankruptcy/compulsory liquidation and sub-categories.
