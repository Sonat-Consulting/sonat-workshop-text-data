



Limited access to the Central Coordinating Register for Legal Entities

Last updated: 7 November 2021.





We provide limited technical access for private actors.

By applying this service, you have access to the following services:

Data basis mini – a service containing organisation number, type of organisation, business name, business address and postal address.
Contact data – a service containing telephone number, telefax number, mobile telephone, e-mail address and website address
Name search – a service that makes it possible to find an organisation number by searching on business name.
Special information – a service containing information about status. You will for example be supplied with information about registration in the Register of Business Enterprises or the VAT Register, or whether the enterprise is under bankruptcy.
Information from the affiliated registers– a service containing information about industrial code, partnership contributions and number of employees.
The Register of Non-Profit Organisations – a service containing date of registration, category, account number, last submitted articles of association with date of registration, last submitted annual accounts and participation in the grassroot allotment programme.
