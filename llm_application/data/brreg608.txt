



Announcements from the Foundation Authority

Last updated: 2 June 2022.





The Foundation Authority announces conversion due to liquidation, merger or demerger. 

In addition, the Authority announces a reduction in capital for non-commercial foundations. Capital reductions for business foundations are announced by the Register of Business Enterprises.
 







 
                                Announcements of conversion for liquidation                            



The announcement includes information about:

the name/business name
the organisation number
the business address
the municipality
the type of organization
the date for the decision on conversion due to liquidation
the date when the decision was approved by the Foundation Authority











 
                                Announcements of conversion for merger                            



The announcement includes information about:

the name/business name of the foundation
the organisation number
the business address
the municipality
the type of organisation

The same information is also shown for the acquired foundation, with the exception of type of organisation. The date of decision for conversion for merger, as well as the date of approval by the Foundation Authority are also included.










 
                                Announcements of conversion for demerger                            



The announcement includes information about:

the name/business name
the organisation number
the business address
the municipality
the type of organisation

The same information is also shown for the acquired foundation, with the exception of type of organisation. The date of decision for conversion for demerger, as well as the date of approval by the Foundation Authority are also included.










 
                                Announcements of capital reduction                            



The announcement includes information about:

the name/business name
the organisation number
the business address
the municipality
the type of organisation
the date of the decision of capital reduction
the amount (previous and current)
the purpose the amount is going to be applied to
the date of approval by the Foundation Authority











 
                                How do I find the announcements?                            



If you know the name of the foundation, you can search directly for the name or organisation number on our announcement pages. Alternatively, you can search through advanced search:
Demerger, merger, capital reduction (non-commercial foundations) or dissolution
Select announcement type – From the Foundation Authority
–  Subcategory 1 – select demerger, merger, capital reduction or dissolution
Capital reduction in business foundations
Select announcement type – Changes
– Subcategory 1 –  Capital/financial instruments
– Subcategory 2 – Capital reduction
