



Statistics from The Register of Reporting Obligations of Enterprises

Last updated: 31 January 2022.





This register contains an updated overview of the business community's reporting obligations and shows the possibilities for coordination and simplification.

Here you will find statistics showing the total number of forms, degree of digitalisation both totally and in the individual agency, and load estimate total and divided on agency, as well as the reason for the change in the load. The graphs show status at the end of the year, except for the last column which shows status by date.
