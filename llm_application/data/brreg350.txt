Last updated: 7 November 2021.





Through this service you can order our products machineably. The service is a web service that is not intended for mass posting. 

Through machinably access to the ordering solution, you can order our products electronically. The service is not intended for mass searches or parallel queries.
Fees
A yearly fixed fee for the use of this service is NOK 25,000.00.
Content of the service and technical documentation
You must develop the application that you connect to the ordering solution. We do not provide technical assistance in this area.
Here you will find more information about the service.
How to gain access
In order to gain access to the service, you must submit an e-mail to us.
After signing the agreement, you will be assigned a username and password that you must use to be connected to the solution.
Contact us
For further details about the service, please send us an e-mail.
