


How much is the late filing penalty?





Use the fee calculator to estimate the late filing penalty. 









 
                                The late filing penalty week by week                            




the late filing penalty increases with NOK 1 243 per week the first 8 weeks
the late filing penalty increases with NOK 2 486 per week the next 10 weeks
the late filing penalty increases with NOK 3 729 per week the last 8 weeks

A started week is considered a whole week.
The late filing penalty will increase for a maximum of 26 weeks.
The late filing penalty is based upon the court fee, which is NOK 1 243 for the time being.
