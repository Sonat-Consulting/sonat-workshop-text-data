



How do I delete my role?

Last updated: 17 January 2022.





Here you will find an overview of how to delete various roles that are registered with us. 









 
                                How can I withdraw from my role in a limited company?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation. When withdrawing, you waive all rights and obligations related to your role. You can do this in the form Coordinated register notification. When the resignation has been registered, both the limited company and you who withdraw, will be notified about this.










 
                                How can I resign my role in the association?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation. When you resign, you waive all rights and obligations belonging to your role. You can do this through the form Coordinated register notification. Once the resignation has been registered, both the association and you who resign will be notified.










 
                                How can I withdraw from my role in a co-operate?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation. When withdrawing, you waive all rights and obligations related to your role. You can do this in the form Coordinated register notification. When the resignation has been registered, both the co-operate and you who withdraw, will be notified about this.










 
                                How can I withdraw from my role in a general partnership?

When the resignation has been registered, both the company and you who withdraw, will be notified about this.










 
                                How can I withdraw from my role in a joint ownership according to the law of property?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation. When withdrawing, you waive all rights and obligations related to your role. You can do this in the form Coordinated register notification. When the resignation has been registered, both the joint ownership and you who withdraw, will be notified about this.










 
                                How can I withdraw from my role in a housing cooperative?                            



All role holders can report their own resignation by submitting the form Coordinated register notification.
When withdrawing, you waive all rights and obligations related to your role.
Upon notification of resignation as a board or deputy board member, you must confirm that the housing cooperative has been informed.
When the withdrawal is registered, both the housing cooperative and you who withdraw, will be notified about this.










 
                                How can I withdraw from my role in a condominium flat owner?

When the resignation has been registered, both the condominium and you who withdraw, will be notified about this.
